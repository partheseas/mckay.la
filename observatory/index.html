Get Observatory

Observatory is a modern cross-platform disk usage analyzer. It utilizes a radial sunburst graph for it's primary display element to make it painless and simple to piece together exactly what is taking up space on your computer.

Under the hood it uses Electron, React, and Canvas2D. What this means for most people is that the building blocks underneath are modern, robust, and have a clear upgrade path for future development.
