Adoption

Funding puppy mills creates lots of problems

Dogs are basically the new baby

  • Doesn't need clothing, school supplies, college tuition, or even a bedroom
  • Regular exercise
  • Consistent living space
  • Toys to play with
  • Love, attention, and cuddles
