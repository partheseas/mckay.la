How do video games affect child development?

McKayla Washburn

They are beneficial!

  • Improve motor skills
  • Increase social skills
  • Development of problem solving and team working abilities

They are problematic!

  • Potential to increase behavioral problems
  • Some evidence of a negative effect on social skills
  • Addicitive behavior has sometimes been seen

They are

  • Communicate with your child about reasonable video game consumption
  • All things are best in moderation, and there is no reason to think that video games should be any different.
  • Video games are not as destructive as some may be inclined to believe, but do come with some risks. Parents should observe and communicate with their children to find a healthy balance.
